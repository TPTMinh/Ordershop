About Us

Nice to meet you! We are Project 3Di - an independent Vietnamese VTuber group. To bring you entertaining moments under various activities such as livestream, gaming, singing… on YouTube platform is our ultimate goal! Our name 3Di originated from “Three Dimensions” - with the settings of our VTubers revolving around Three Worlds「三界」: Celestial Realm, Human Region and Underworld. 3Di consists of unique individuals gathered to share one mutual passion. We hope to have you as our companion on the upcoming journey!
